About Us

Burrito Masala is a food cart located in southwest Portland, Oregon. We specialize in making Mexican food with an Indian flair! The fusion of the two rich cultures is a taste that can't be beat. We also know how important it is to get what you want, so we offer a fully customizable menu. You can order your burrito or bowl just as you want it with a variety of options.

How to Order

Choose a burrito or bowl:

  • 1.Choose one meat or veggie option
  • 2.Choose one rice option
  • 3.Choose your condiments
  • 4. Enjoy!
